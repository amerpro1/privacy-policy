Privacy Policy - MultiBox

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob, which may collect anonymous data such as device information and advertising identifiers.

4. Data Security

All data remains on your device and is not stored on external servers.

5. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
